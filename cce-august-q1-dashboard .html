Executive Summary

Q1 FY25 Performance Overview: The APAC & ME region delivered strong results across all marketing channels, demonstrating the effectiveness of our integrated content and customer engagement strategy.

CRM Excellence: Our email marketing programmes maintained exceptional delivery performance at 98.4%, with 29.5M emails sent across the quarter. Triggered campaigns significantly outperformed manual sends, particularly in Japan and Australia markets. The key challenge remains improving manual campaign CTRs, which averaged below 0.2%.

Organic Growth Success: SEO traffic reached 1.78M visits with Japan dominating at 56% of total traffic. Singapore showed exceptional growth with 20% QoQ increase, whilst Australia and UAE faced conversion challenges despite stable traffic. The content production remained strong with 120 pieces delivered across all markets.

Social Media Momentum: Social channels demonstrated remarkable engagement growth with 48.4% increase in interactions and 56% boost in link clicks. Japan achieved an exceptional 774.9% increase in link clicks, while Australia saw 114.7% engagement growth. The collaborative approach with local markets and platform-specific content strategy is yielding measurable returns, maintaining engagement quality whilst expanding reach.

Executive Summary

Performance: Q1's performance shows stable audience numbers but struggling conversion mechanics. A strong demo-to-first-trade conversion supports that when users reach the demo stage, the experience is highly effective. The key insight is that APAC has a strong "bottom of funnel" (demo to trade) but a lacking "middle of funnel" (education to demo). This suggests strategy should focus on getting educational content users to trial the demo experience.

Data Measurement Framework: Working with Data Analytics, Education is developing a new reliable performance measurement framework, to track KPIs and ultimately prove educational content effectiveness and value to business, resolving data doubt. Initial reports have offered critical data regarding user's engagement with IG Academy and corresponding trading behaviours.

Course Launches and Development: "Tools for Traders" and "A beginner's guide to forex trading" currently being uploaded for Singapore. "Placing Your First Trade: A Beginner's Guide" is live in Australia and being uploaded in UAE. "Charting Essentials," a new advanced course for experienced traders, is in design.

Key Performance Insights

  • Japan leads with 8.5K total users (45% of divisional total) and highest FT CVR at 3.06%
  • Australia shows strong engagement with 9K sessions and 5.6K total users
  • UAE has lowest conversion at 0.41% FT CVR, indicating opportunity for improvement
  • Overall demo-to-first-trade conversion strong, but education-to-demo needs focus
