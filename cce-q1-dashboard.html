Executive Summary

Q1 FY25 Performance Overview: The APAC & ME region delivered strong results across all marketing channels, demonstrating the effectiveness of our integrated content and customer engagement strategy.

CRM Excellence: Our email marketing programmes maintained exceptional delivery performance at 98.4%, with 29.5M emails sent across the quarter. Triggered campaigns significantly outperformed manual sends, particularly in Japan and Australia markets. The key challenge remains improving manual campaign CTRs, which averaged below 0.2%.

Organic Growth Success: SEO traffic reached 1.78M visits with Japan dominating at 56% of total traffic. Singapore showed exceptional growth with 20% QoQ increase, whilst Australia and UAE faced conversion challenges despite stable traffic. The content production remained strong with 120 pieces delivered across all markets.

Social Media Momentum: Social channels demonstrated remarkable engagement growth with 48.4% increase in interactions and 56% boost in link clicks. The collaborative approach with local markets and platform-specific content strategy is yielding measurable returns, maintaining engagement quality whilst expanding reach.
